Getting started

Welcome to NOVA community.

Your participation will help identify and strengthen ideas for our company.

Turning your ideas to life

You can submit your ideas to Campaigns. If you have an idea that doesn't fit into a campaign, you can submit it at any time under the Spontaneous idea category also on the Home page.

Idea submission

When you submit an idea, NOVA will notify you if other similar ideas have been submitted so you can collaborate with others who've had similar insights.

You can either leave your submission (which will be saved as draft) and contribute to one of the suggested ideas, or continue the process and publish your own.

Once you submit an idea, you become the Idea Champion for your idea and you can put the power of the community to work with you.

Voting and Commenting

When you review a new idea, you can join the conversation by commenting on it and suggesting ways in which it may be improved.

The ability to post anonymously or reply to existing comments, enhances this process.

In addition to writing comments, you can vote a rate or like ideas you think are good for our company and worthy of further development.

Innovation Points

Whenever you take action in NOVA, you earn Innovator points. Points are awarded for submitting ideas, commenting on others' ideas, voting, etc.

As you accrue points, you attain a certain Innovator status. This status is an indicator of your activity in the community and your entrepreneurial insights.

In addition to listening your innovator status on your resume. they are also shown on the homepage or inside the campaign.

How good ideas become great

Ideas that are implemented typically become significantly different than what was originally submitted. The best ideas are challenged, changed, combined and modified by the time they make it through the approval process.

The most important thing you can do as an Idea Champion is to be an interested advocate for your idea and seek as much input as you can from this unique community of talented individuals.

As much as your idea will be strengthened by seeking comments from others, be sure to offer others the power of your ideas, insights and suggestions as well.
